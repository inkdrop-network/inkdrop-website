What is InkDrop?

InkDrop is set out to become the first decentralized microblogging platform that pays its content creators, curators and consumers. The platform is designed around the DROP crypto token.
With the right mechanisms in place and a fine tuned incentive system the InkDrop is designed to be self-regulating. Thus, fostering positive network behaviour and punishing trolls and spammers misusing the platform.

Got DROP?

DROPs are utility tokens, with a token reward system designed specifically for a social media, broadcasting networks. DROPs will be used inside the InkDrop ecosystem and help to monetize user generated value and incentivize positive user behavior.
Once InkDrop goes live, DROP tokens can be earned on the platform or traded on exchanges. At the time of writing it is still undecided whether or not DROPs will be generated through an ICO or not.

Proof-of-Care

Proof-of-Care is a novel approach to achieve a self-regulating social network. On InkDrop the community itself is empowered to enforce the network’s terms of service. Users can earn DROP tokens in exchange for moderating the platform, allowing for an easy access into the crypto ecosystem.
The mechanism is based on Vitalik Buterin’s SchellingCoin approach and aims to produce the community’s most objective opinion towards the compliance of a post.
