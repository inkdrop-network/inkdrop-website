What is InkDrop?

InkDrop is set out to become the first decentralized microblogging platform that pays its content creators, curators and consumers. The platform is designed around the DROP crypto token, which enables a series of unique new features.
The InkDrop social network is designed to be self-regulating with its fine tuned incentive system and innovative social mechanisms. Thus, fostering positive behaviour and punishing trolls and spammers that try to misuse the platform.

Got DROP?

DROPs are utility tokens for a token reward system designed specifically for a social media broadcasting network. DROPs will be used inside the InkDrop ecosystem to unlock all its features, enable the monetization of user generated content, and incentivize positive user behavior.
As soon as InkDrop goes live, DROP tokens can be earned on the platform or traded on exchanges. At the time of writing it is still undecided whether or not DROPs will be generated through an ICO or not.

Proof-of-Care Protocol.
